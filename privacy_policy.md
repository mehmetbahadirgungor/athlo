# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 22, 2025

**Athlo** ("we," "us," "the App," or "the Service") places great importance on the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you use the Athlo mobile application, how that data is used, protected, and shared.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you accept the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide, improve, and personalize our Services, we may collect the following types of information:

### 1.1. Personal Information
Information you voluntarily provide when creating an account or updating your profile:
- First and last name
- Email address
- Unique user ID (User ID) generated via Firebase Authentication or Google Sign-In

### 1.2. Health and Fitness Data
Data entered by you to enable the App's core functionalities:
- Workout history, plans, and performance records
- Nutrition logs, meal information, and calorie tracking
- Body measurements, weight, height, and personal goals

### 1.3. Device and Usage Information
- Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and other technical information
- Your interactions within the App, features used, usage frequency, and performance data (e.g., crash reports and anonymous usage statistics)

### 1.4. Visual and Media Data
- **Camera and Photos:** Camera and gallery access may be used for barcode scanning (food products) or AI-powered meal recognition features. This data is processed only on your device and used solely to provide the relevant feature. No visual data is uploaded to servers or shared with third parties.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide and operate the Services (workout/nutrition tracking, personalized recommendations)
- Account management and authentication
- To analyze App performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
- To personalize the user experience (AI-powered insights and recommendations)
- To create anonymous and aggregated statistics to improve the App

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.** Your data is shared only on a limited basis with trusted service providers necessary for delivering the Services (listed below).

## 3. App Permissions

The App may request the following permissions to perform its functions:
- **Internet Access:** To synchronize data with our cloud servers (Firebase)
- **Camera:** For scanning food barcodes or analyzing meal photos with AI
- **Photos/Gallery:** To select from existing photos
- **Notifications:** For workout reminders and important updates

These permissions are used only for the stated purposes and can be managed at any time in your device settings.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services for secure storage, authentication, and processing of data:
- **Google Firebase:** For authentication (Authentication), database (Firestore), storage (Storage), serverless functions (Cloud Functions), performance monitoring (Performance Monitoring), crash reporting (Crashlytics), and anonymous usage analytics (Analytics). Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Google Sign-In:** To facilitate account login.

These services process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies. Your data is shared with these providers only to the extent necessary to provide the Services.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (encryption, secure connections - HTTPS/TLS, access controls, and regular security audits) to protect your personal data. Your data is transmitted encrypted and stored on secure servers. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We continually strive to maintain the highest level of data security.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the Services. When you delete your account, all your personal data, health/fitness records, and related information are permanently removed from our systems.

You have the right to access, correct, or request complete deletion of your data. You can submit such requests through the App ("Settings > Delete My Account") or by contacting us using the details below.

## 7. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- Access and obtain a copy of your data
- Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Object to or restrict data processing

We will review and respond to your requests within a reasonable timeframe (usually within 30 days).

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located in various countries, primarily the United States, as part of Firebase services. Google provides adequate data protection standards (EU Standard Contractual Clauses and other appropriate mechanisms).

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. In the event of significant changes, we will notify you via in-app notification, email, or a prominent notice within the App. We recommend reviewing the updates regularly. The latest version of the Policy can be accessed within the App or on our website.
